# Cost

Dominion uses shared credit accounting for both modes.

| Tier | Intended use | Cost |
| --- | --- | --- |
| fast | default interpretation, routine turns, omens | 1 credit |
| deep | complex diplomacy, major world turns, archive compression | 5 credits |

Every WorldMind call logs purpose, tier, token counts, and credit cost. Scenario creators may lock a world to a tier or allow per-turn choice.
