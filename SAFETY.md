# Safety

Dominion moderates decrees, diplomacy, free-form divine acts, and published worlds before they reach LLM providers or the public hub.

## Blocked

- Real-world hate speech or dehumanization of real ethnic, religious, or national groups.
- Sexual content involving minors.
- Harassment or targeting of real living individuals.
- Glorification of real hate movements outside a factual historical frame.

## Allowed

- Fictional conflict between invented polities.
- Historical atrocity or conflict handled in a factual, analytical register.
- In-world tyranny, religion, conquest, famine, and disaster when not targeting real protected groups or real living people.

## Reporting

Published worlds include in-app reporting. Reports enter an admin review queue and can delist worlds pending review.
